Hey There! I am Tolani Odukoya. I am a Pacesetter, Visionary and a Global Citizen.
Tolani Odukoya is a doctor in training, social entrepreneur and advocate of quality education for sustainable development. He hopes to see a world where every child, regardless of their socio-economic status and background will have access to quality education to better prepare them for the future of work.He is the founding executive director of The Mindset Initiative for Sustainable Education, a youth led, volunteer driven NGO committed to increasing access to quality education in all communities in Nigeria through global citizenship education, mentorship and practical leadership.
OTB is currently the college coordinator of SDGsACT LASU. A non-profit organization with the sole aim of imprinting the sustainable development goals into the heart of every student in Lagos State University. Through his work at SDGsACT LASU, he has been able to create awareness about the UN global goals across the campus.Tolani is the founder of www.otbglobe.com where he inspires and motivates youths towards discovering their passion and purpose for life and also runs documentary stories about medical school and its struggles to help medical students across Nigeria thrive and better informed.
